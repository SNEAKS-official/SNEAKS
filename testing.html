Testing

Feedback & improvements

To help improve our website, we asked a total of 5 people (within our target group) to check out our website and provide us with feedback about its contents, layout, images, and usability.

These were the points that stood out most:

  • LANDING PAGE: Overall this seemed to look okay, but some users mentioned there was something missing like a logo or more colors.
  • INVEST PAGE: There was text missing on this page, which confused users and made them unable to understand what they’d invest in. We updated this and added clickable buttons.
  • ABOUT PAGE: A lot of users mentioned that the pictures didn’t fit the screen. We fixed this on both mobile and desktop. Also; the text which appeared on top of the carousel was too big and distracting, so we got rid of it.
  • MARKETING PAGE: The ‘objectives’ seem to look weird to the users. We changed these from stating them next to each other to stating them on top of each other. This looks better and more intuitive.
  • SCREEN FIT: Users mentioned that on laptop/desktop everything fits, but that on mobile devices some pictures don’t fit well. We tried fixing this as much as possible, but due to lack of technical skills some pictures might still not fit perfectly on mobile devices. This would be fixable with more time and more technical skills.
