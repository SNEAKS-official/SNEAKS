Brand & Visual Identity

SNEAKS represents a sense of ease, a casual vibe. We wanted to use a simple logo that could possibly also be used as a sticker or something alike. The big S in the middle tries to get the consumer interested in reading what the letter stands for. We hope that the consumer will read all text in the logo. We chose to use the word ‘supplies’ instead of ‘sneakers’ to keep a level of mysteriousness around the brand. We hope that the consumer becomes intrigued to know what is meant with ‘supplies’ and decides to click on the link that leads to our landing page.

For our brand’s identity, we will use the following beautiful colors:

#7DAFE0 - Light blue - We used this vibrant blue color, also described as ‘sky blue’ as the primary color for our brand. The reason why is because this color blue stands out from other colors, because of it’s brightness. BUT, at the same time, it is also soft enough not to be considered an intense color. Also, it matches well with styles/outfits that are worn often in the streetwear scene, so it subliminally links back to the industry.

#F6F3E8 - Beige - This color is used as a complimentary color for the primary blue color. The light beige color matches super well with the sky blue color, creating a beautifully soft color palette, while still making the brand look professional. Also, beige colors are very often used as a primary/secondary tone in streetwear. Again, linking back to the industry!

#EBECED - Soft gray - Not used very often, but this soft gray color is used when the light blue or beige colors don’t fit in a specific situation. We chose this color because it doesn’t attract attention, and is just as soft as the blue and beige colors.
